What is a pico press?

When a small press is too large, a micro press is too macro, and a nano press is too giga, a pico press might just be the right size.

We make books the way you make breakfast at home: unpredictably and in small quantities.

Glorious Breakfast Press believes that great books can come from even the smallest of publishers.

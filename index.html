About the Press

What is a pico press?

Smaller than a small press. Smaller than a micro press. A pico press makes books the way you make breakfast for someone you love: in small quantities, at odd hours, with an unreasonable amount of attention to detail.

We publish a handful of titles a year in tiny editions — chapbooks, zines, and pamphlets that are printed, folded, and sewn by hand. When an edition is gone, it's gone, like the last piece of bacon.

Glorious Breakfast Press believes that a book doesn't have to be long to stay with you, and that the best reading happens in the quiet part of the morning, before the day gets its hands on you.
